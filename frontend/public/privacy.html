Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Social Buster ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Social Buster platform at socialbuster.com, an AI-powered social media marketing tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service.

By creating an account or connecting a social media platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1a. Account Information

  • Email address and password (password is hashed and stored by Supabase Auth — we never see or store your plaintext password)
  • Brand profile details you provide: brand name, industry, target audience, brand voice description

1b. Social Media Platform Data

When you connect a social media account (Facebook, Instagram, or other supported platforms), we collect:

  • OAuth access tokens — encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage. Used solely to act on your behalf (publish posts, read comments, send messages).
  • Page/account identifiers — your Page ID and account name, used to route content to the correct destination.
  • Post engagement data — likes, comments, shares, reach, and impressions on posts you publish through our platform. Used to provide you with performance analytics.
  • Comments on your posts — text, author name, and timestamp of comments on posts published through Social Buster, received via real-time webhook notifications from the platform. Used to power the comment-to-DM automation feature you configure.
  • Direct message conversations — when you enable DM automation, we send messages on your behalf and store conversation state (message text, timestamps, and any information the recipient voluntarily provides such as email, phone number, or name). We only message users who have interacted with your content.

1c. Content You Create

  • Briefs, generated posts, captions, hashtags, and calls-to-action
  • Media files you select from your connected Google Drive — we read your files directly from Google Drive and do not permanently store them on our servers. When you attach a Drive file to a post, a temporary copy is held in cloud-hosted storage (Supabase Storage) only for the duration of the publishing process. Once the post is published or deleted, the temporary copy is removed. We do not retain your Drive media beyond what is needed to complete the publish.
  • AI-generated images created through the platform — stored in cloud-hosted storage (Supabase Storage), not on our application servers. You can delete these at any time from your media library.
  • Video clips you select from your media library — temporary processed copies are held in cloud-hosted storage during publishing, then removed.
  • DM automation rules you configure (trigger keywords, message templates, follow-up sequences)

1d. Billing Information

  • Subscription plan and status only. All payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. Your credit card number, CVV, expiration date, and full billing address are entered directly on Stripe's secure payment form and are never transmitted to, processed by, or stored on Social Buster's servers. We only receive a confirmation of your subscription plan and payment status from Stripe — we never have access to your payment card details at any point.

1e. Usage and Log Data

  • Feature usage timestamps, API request logs, and error logs — used to maintain, debug, and improve the service.
  • Session identifiers: When you log in, we generate a unique session ID stored in your browser. If you log in from a new device or browser, your previous session is automatically invalidated — only one active session per account is permitted at a time. We store only your current active session ID. We do not collect device fingerprints, browsing history, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Publish content — to create and publish posts to your connected social media accounts on your behalf, at the times you schedule.
  • Generate AI content — to produce post suggestions, captions, hashtags, and images based on the briefs and preferences you provide.
  • Monitor comments — to receive real-time notifications from the platform when someone comments on your published posts, and check those comments for trigger keywords you define so the DM automation you configured can respond.
  • Send direct messages — to send automated DMs on your behalf to users who comment on your posts with trigger phrases you set. Messages are only sent within each platform's allowed messaging window (24 hours for Meta platforms).
  • Collect leads — when a user responds to your automated DM with information (email, phone, name), we store that data in your account so you can export it.
  • Show performance analytics — to display engagement metrics, posting recommendations, and intelligence insights based on your own post performance.
  • Improve recommendations — we anonymously compile and compare performance metrics across users to improve AI-generated suggestions. For example, we calculate average engagement rates by industry, best posting times by region, and effective content formats by niche. This data is stripped of all identifying information before aggregation — your post content, brand name, account details, and personal information are never included. See Section 5 for full details on what is and is not included in aggregated data.
  • Communicate with you — to send account-related notifications, system alerts, and responses to support requests.

4. DM Automation and Messaging

Social Buster includes a comment-to-DM automation feature. Here is exactly how it works and what data is involved:

Important: AI is used only to suggest post content (captions, hooks, hashtags). You review, edit, and approve every post and every DM message template before anything is published or sent. No content is ever posted or messaged without your explicit approval.

  1. You configure triggers — for each post, you define trigger keywords (e.g., "interested," "info") and write the message templates to send. These are your words, not AI-generated messages.
  2. We monitor comments — our system receives real-time webhook notifications from the platform when someone comments on your published posts, and checks those comments for your trigger keywords.
  3. We send DMs on your behalf — when a match is found, we send the message template you wrote from your connected Page/account to the commenter. We respect each platform's 24-hour messaging window policy.
  4. Lead collection — if your automation includes follow-up questions (e.g., "What's your email?"), the recipient's responses are stored as lead data in your account. You can export this data as CSV.
  5. Rate limits — we enforce daily sending limits (100 DMs/day for Facebook, 80 DMs/day for Instagram) to protect your account from platform restrictions.

Recipient data: We store the commenter's platform user ID, name (as displayed on the platform), and any information they voluntarily provide in the DM conversation (email, phone, name, or custom fields you asked for). This data is only visible to you and is never shared with other Social Buster users or third parties.

5. Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We anonymously compile and compare performance metrics across users to improve AI-generated content recommendations for all users. This is strictly limited to numerical performance data and never includes personal information or content.

What IS included in aggregated data:

  • Average engagement rates (likes, comments, shares, reach) grouped by industry, platform, and post type
  • Best performing posting times grouped by niche and geographic region
  • Effective content formats and topics (e.g., "video posts in the fitness industry average 2x more engagement than image posts")
  • Average conversion rates for DM automations by industry (e.g., "retail brands see a 12% lead conversion rate")

What is NEVER included in aggregated data:

  • Your brand name, business name, or account name
  • Your email address or any personal contact information
  • Your media files (images, videos)
  • Your DM message content or lead data
  • Your OAuth tokens or platform credentials
  • Any information that could identify you or your business

This data is always aggregated and anonymized before it is used. No individual user's data can be identified or reverse-engineered from aggregated metrics. Aggregated data is never sold or shared with third parties — it is used solely to improve recommendations within the Social Buster platform.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Database: Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (a cloud-hosted service), with Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring each user can only access their own data. Your data is isolated from all other users at the database level.
  • Media files: Media (images, videos) is not stored on our application servers. We read media directly from your connected Google Drive — we do not copy or retain your Drive files beyond what is temporarily needed during publishing. Temporary copies are held in cloud-hosted storage (Supabase Storage) only for the duration of the publishing process and are removed immediately after. AI-generated images are stored in Supabase cloud storage until you delete them. We do not maintain a permanent copy of your uploaded or Drive media on our infrastructure.
  • OAuth tokens: All platform access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage. Encryption keys are stored separately from the database.
  • Passwords: Handled entirely by Supabase Auth using bcrypt hashing. We never store or have access to plaintext passwords.
  • Payments: All credit card numbers, CVV codes, expiration dates, and billing details are processed and stored exclusively by Stripe. No payment card information ever touches our servers. We only store your subscription plan name and payment status.
  • Personal data: Your personal information (email, brand details, post content, lead data, DM conversations) is never sold, rented, shared with, or made accessible to any third party, other Social Buster user, or external organization — except for the third-party services listed in Section 9 that are necessary to operate the platform, and only the minimum data required for each service.
  • Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with standard industry protections (firewalls, access controls, encrypted connections via HTTPS).

7. Data Retention

All personally identifiable data is deleted on a rolling 90-day basis once it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected. This means that data associated with deleted posts, closed DM conversations, removed automations, and disconnected platform connections is permanently purged within 90 days of deletion or disconnection — not held indefinitely.

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 90 days.
  • Post content and briefs: The text content of your posts and briefs (captions, hooks, hashtags, etc.) is retained until you delete it or close your account. We do not retain your media files. We read media directly from your connected Google Drive — files are only temporarily copied to our storage during the publishing process and are removed immediately after publishing completes. AI-generated images are stored in Supabase cloud storage (not on our application servers) and are retained until you delete them from your media library or close your account.
  • DM conversations and leads: Retained until you delete them or delete your account, then purged within 90 days.
  • Comments: Comments on your posts are retained while your account is active to power sentiment analysis and intelligence features. When you delete your account or revoke Meta access, comments are anonymized — the commenter's name, handle, platform comment ID, and link to the original post are permanently removed. The comment text, sentiment classification, and platform remain as anonymous, de-identified data points indefinitely to improve recommendations for all users. No individual user or commenter can be identified from this anonymized data. See Section 8 for full details on account deletion.
  • Log data: Retained for up to 30 days, then automatically purged.
  • Aggregated metrics: Retained indefinitely as they contain no personally identifiable information.

8. User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your data at any time through either of these methods:

  • In-app message: Send a message through the Messages section inside your Social Buster account with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Please be aware that because much of the data we collect is anonymized and aggregated, a deletion request may result in full account deletion. We may or may not send a confirmation — processing will be completed within 90 days of your request.
  • Platform revocation: You can revoke Social Buster's access to your Facebook/Instagram data at any time via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites. This immediately revokes our access tokens — we can no longer access your platform data.

Automatic deauthorization handling: When you revoke Social Buster's access via Facebook or Instagram settings, Meta sends us a deauthorization callback. We automatically process this by deleting: DM conversations, collected lead data, DM automation configurations, connected platform tokens, and any direct messaging history. Your posts, briefs, media library, user profile, and billing data are preserved — these were created by you on our platform, not obtained via Meta's APIs.

Comment data: Comments on your posts are authored by third-party users (the people who commented), not by you. When you revoke access, we anonymize these comments by removing the commenter's handle, the platform comment ID, and the link to the original post. The comment text, sentiment classification, and platform remain as anonymous, de-identified data points used to improve our collective intelligence engine for all users. No individual commenter can be identified from this anonymized data.

Full account deletion: If you request full account deletion, we will remove all data tied to your account — including your account, posts, briefs, media, leads, DM conversations, and anonymized comment data — within 90 days of the request.

Anonymized aggregated metrics (performance benchmarks, cohort averages) that have already been computed are not reversed, as they contain no identifying information.

10. Legacy Membership and Affiliate Program

Social Buster operates a referral-based affiliate program exclusively available to Legacy members — a limited founding-member subscription tier. This section describes how referral and commission data is collected, used, and retained.

10a. Who Can Participate

Only active Legacy members are eligible to participate in the affiliate program. Standard subscribers (Free Trial, Starter, Professional, Buster) cannot earn affiliate commissions. Legacy membership is offered in limited cohorts at a price determined by Social Buster and is never priced at more than 50% of the highest available combined retail subscription price at the time of the cohort's launch.

10b. Referral Tracking

Each Legacy member who joins the affiliate program is assigned a unique referral slug (a short link identifier). When a new user signs up through your referral link, your account is permanently recorded as the referring affiliate for that user. This referral assignment is permanent and cannot be changed, transferred, or reassigned — for any reason, by anyone, including Social Buster staff. The referring affiliate earns applicable commissions on that user for the lifetime of their subscription.

We store the following referral data in your account:

  • Your referral slug and the signup date of each referred user
  • Commission amounts earned, pending, and paid out
  • Payout history including amounts, dates, and Stripe Connect transfer IDs
  • Any clawback or reserve activity related to chargebacks or refunds

10c. Commission Eligibility Rules

Commissions are only paid on qualifying referrals. The following referrals do not generate commissions:

  • Free Trial accounts — no subscription fee means no commission basis
  • Other Legacy member accounts — Legacy members referring other Legacy members do not earn commissions on those referrals
  • Any account that has not made a qualifying paid subscription payment

Commission rates and caps are set by Social Buster and may be updated over time. Your Legacy membership agreement will specify the applicable rate at the time you join.

10d. Payout Requirements and Forfeiture Rules

To receive a commission payout, your own Legacy membership subscription must be current and in good standing at the time of payout. Payouts are processed on the 15th of each month for commissions earned in the prior calendar month.

Commissions are forfeited (permanently lost, not deferred) if any of the following occur within a 12-month rolling period:

  • Your Legacy subscription is paused, canceled, or suspended
  • Your Legacy subscription remains unpaid for more than 35 days
  • Your account is flagged for fraud, abuse, or violation of our Terms of Service

Forfeited commissions are not reinstated if your subscription is later reactivated. Commissions earned after reactivation accrue normally going forward.

10e. Reserve Withholding

To protect against chargebacks and refunds, we withhold a reserve from each payout. The reserve percentage is set by Social Buster and disclosed in your affiliate dashboard. Reserves are released 60 days after the payout date, provided no chargeback or refund has been initiated against the underlying subscription. If a chargeback or refund occurs, the corresponding commission may be clawed back from future payouts or from the held reserve.

10f. Stripe Connect and Payout Processing

Affiliate payouts are processed via Stripe Connect. To receive payouts, you must complete Stripe's identity verification and onboarding process on their hosted platform. Social Buster does not collect or store your banking details, tax identification numbers, or identity documents — these are provided directly to Stripe and governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy. We store only your Stripe Connect account ID and payout records.

10g. Program Modification and Termination

Social Buster reserves the right to modify commission rates, eligibility rules, or payout terms with 30 days' written notice to active affiliates. Social Buster reserves the right to terminate the affiliate program entirely, in which case all pending commissions for qualifying referrals at the time of termination will be paid out in the final payout cycle, subject to the eligibility and forfeiture rules above.
