Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Social Buster ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Social Buster platform, a social media management tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Social media connections: OAuth access tokens for platforms you connect (Facebook, Instagram, etc.). Tokens are encrypted before storage and never shared.
  • Content you create: Posts, captions, hashtags, and media files you upload or generate through the platform.
  • Usage data: Log data such as timestamps and feature usage, used only to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.
  • To generate AI-assisted post suggestions based on your briefs and preferences.
  • To display performance metrics from your connected accounts.
  • To send you notifications related to your account and scheduled posts.

3. Facebook and Meta Platform Data

When you connect your Facebook or Instagram account, we access only the data necessary to publish posts and read engagement metrics on your behalf. We use the following permissions:

  • pages_manage_posts — to create and publish posts to your Facebook Page.
  • pages_manage_videos — to upload and publish video posts to your Facebook Page.
  • pages_read_engagement — to read engagement metrics on your posts.
  • pages_show_list — to list your Facebook Pages during the connection process.

We do not sell, share, or use your Facebook or Instagram data for any purpose other than providing the Social Buster service to you.

4. Data Storage and Security

All OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored in our database. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your data at any time. To do so, email us at mark@markseminko.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.

If you connected Social Buster through Facebook, you can also revoke access at any time via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Social Buster:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Meta Graph API — Facebook and Instagram publishing
  • Groq / OpenAI-compatible APIs — AI content generation
  • Cloudflare Workers AI — AI image generation

8. Children's Privacy

Social Buster is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
